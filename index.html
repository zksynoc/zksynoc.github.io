Zksync™ | Official Site

Zksync is a Layer-2 scaling solution for Ethereum that utilizes cutting-edge zero-knowledge (ZK) technology to enhance scalability while preserving the foundational values of Ethereum - freedom, self-sovereignty, and decentralization. It aims to address the scalability challenges faced by Ethereum, enabling faster and more cost-effective transactions without compromising security.

Key Features and Benefits of ZKSync:

1. Scalability: ZKSync significantly increases Ethereum's throughput by aggregating multiple transactions into a single proof, reducing the load on the Ethereum mainnet. This allows for faster and more efficient transaction processing, enabling a higher number of transactions per second.

2. Low Fees: By leveraging ZK technology, ZKSync achieves low transaction fees compared to the Ethereum mainnet. This makes it more accessible for users and reduces the cost of interacting with decentralized applications (dApps).

3. Security: ZKSync prioritizes security and ensures the same level of trust as the Ethereum mainnet. It achieves this through the use of zero-knowledge proofs, which allow for the verification of transaction validity without revealing sensitive information.

4. Decentralization: ZKSync maintains the ethos of Ethereum by preserving decentralization. It achieves this by relying on Ethereum's security guarantees and utilizing ZK technology to scale while keeping the majority of the transaction data on-chain.

5. Compatibility: ZKSync is fully compatible with existing Ethereum smart contracts and dApps. Developers can seamlessly integrate their applications with ZKSync, leveraging its scalability benefits without the need for significant modifications.

6. Ecosystem Adoption: ZKSync has gained significant traction within the Ethereum ecosystem. Over 200 projects, including prominent names like Chainlink, Uniswap, Aave, and many others, have expressed interest in deploying on ZKSync.

How ZKSync Works:

  • Commitment Phase: In this phase, users deposit their funds into a ZKSync smart contract on the Ethereum mainnet. These funds are then locked and become available for use within the ZKSync Layer-2 environment.
  • Transition Phase: During this phase, users can perform various transactions within the ZKSync Layer-2 environment, such as transfers, swaps, and interactions with dApps. These transactions are processed off-chain, ensuring scalability and low fees.
  • Withdrawal Phase: If users wish to move their funds back to the Ethereum mainnet, they initiate a withdrawal request. The ZKSync smart contract verifies the validity of the request and releases the funds on the Ethereum mainnet.

zkSync Era and zkSync Lite?

zkSync Era and zkSync Lite are two different versions of the zkSync scaling solution for Ethereum. Here's a breakdown of the two:

zkSync Lite:

  • zkSync Lite is a standalone scaling solution designed to enable fast, cheap, and secure transactions on the Ethereum network.

  • It utilizes Zero-Knowledge proofs technology to compress transaction data and batch them into larger blocks, reducing the amount of gas required and increasing transaction throughput.

  • With zkSync Lite, users can send transactions on Ethereum with improved efficiency and lower costs compared to traditional Ethereum transactions.

    • zkSync Era:

  • zkSync Era is the next version of zkSync that is currently in development.
  • It is expected to offer more advanced features such as support for decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs) and non-fungible tokens (NFTs).
  • zkSync Era aims to provide even faster transaction times and lower gas fees compared to zkSync Lite.

  • Unlike zkSync Lite, zkSync Era does not require an activation fee.

  • However, currently, zkSync Era does not allow the use of STORJ tokens to pay for transaction fees. Instead, users need to deposit ETH to their zkSync Era account to withdraw to any L1 address.
